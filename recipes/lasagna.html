Lasagna

Description

This easy lasagna recipe has been in development since my 7th grade home economics class. Over the years the recipe has been changed probably over a hundred times, but in the last 5 years it has not changed a single time. Itâ€™s finally gotten to a place that it is truly the Ultimate Meat Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 teaspons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 2 teaspoons Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper falkes
  • 6 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 box lasagna noodles (Do not boil)
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1/2 pound of Provolone cheese (sliced)
  • 1/2 pound mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a large pot, brown the beef in one large piece until it is browned on the bottom, 2-3minutes, breaking apart the beef into slightly larger chunks.
  3. Cook unitl the beef is browned and add in the basil, oregano, 2 teaspons Kosher Salt, crushed red pepper flakes and fennel seeds.
  4. Stir until you start to smeel the fennel seeds starting to toast then drain the fat and add in the mariana sauce.
  5. In your ricotta container, if you hae space, add the egg and 1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt and mix well.
  6. Add your no boil noodles to the meat sauce ( you are not looking to cook them, just coat them in the sauce to ensure they don't get dry in the oven).
  7. Put the 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking pan ( use one with straight sides for easy assembly and for added height. If you are using a glass baking pan with curved sides it may not be large enough to fit this quanity of food).
  8. Add the dried no boil noodles in an even layer, for this pan it was 5 noodles per layer, four across and one broken in half lengthwise to go down the side of the pan.
  9. Add 1/3 the provolone slices, another 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce, 1/4 of the mozzarella, 1/4 of the parmesan and 1/3 of the ricotta in spoonfuls dotting across the pan.
  10. Repeat with the same noodle/cheese/meat sauce process.
  11. Finally, add a top layer of lasagna noodles and cover with remaining Parmesan and mozzarella. Cover with foil.
  12. Bake for 40 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional ten minutes to brown the cheese.
  13. Let cool for 20-25 minutes before serving.
  14. Garnish with parsley.
